# sprite Is Following Path

Returns true if the sprite is currently following a path,
and false otherwise

```sig
scene.spriteIsFollowingPath(null)
```

## Parameters

* **sprite**: A sprite to check if it is currently following a path.

## Returns

* True if the sprite is currently following a path, and false otherwise.

## Example

Creates a sprite that will constantly say if it's true or false that it's following a path.

```blocks
let mySprite = sprites.create(img`
    . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . 
    . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . 
    . . . . . . . . . b 5 5 b . . . 
    . . . . . . b b b b b b . . . . 
    . . . . . b b 5 5 5 5 5 b . . . 
    . b b b b b 5 5 5 5 5 5 5 b . . 
    . b d 5 b 5 5 5 5 5 5 5 5 b . . 
    . . b 5 5 b 5 d 1 f 5 d 4 f . . 
    . . b d 5 5 b 1 f f 5 4 4 c . . 
    b b d b 5 5 5 d f b 4 4 4 4 b . 
    b d d c d 5 5 b 5 4 4 4 4 4 4 b 
    c d d d c c b 5 5 5 5 5 5 5 b . 
    c b d d d d d 5 5 5 5 5 5 5 b . 
    . c d d d d d d 5 5 5 5 5 d b . 
    . . c b d d d d d 5 5 5 b b . . 
    . . . c c c c c c c c b b . . . 
    `, SpriteKind.Player)
game.onUpdate(() => {
    mySprite.say(scene.spriteIsFollowingPath(mySprite))
})
```
